5. Commissions and Payments

BeTogether applies a commission fee on all transactions:

Payments are processed through the secure in-app payment system.

6. Funds Management and Withdrawals

Payments received for services are shown in the account balance of the provider. Funds are available for withdrawal 60 minutes after the service has started. This ensures protection for both the service seeker and the provider. In case of disputes, funds may be frozen until resolved.

If the service is not provided due to the provider’s fault, the seeker is entitled to a full refund.
